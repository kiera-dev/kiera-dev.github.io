Star Trek Online

Over a nearly three-year span, I spearheaded data optimization efforts. I also developed interactive dashboards for real-time project insights and conducted in-depth behavioral analysis of microtransactions to boost revenue. Battle Pirates

As a Mobile Analyst, I contributed to Fair Play anti-abuse initiatives for Battle Pirates, leveraging advanced data analysis techniques on massive datasets to detect and deter cheating behaviors. Through the development of SQL queries in BigQuery, I successfully reduced the incorrect flagging of user data by 15%, significantly minimizing false positives. These queries were later adapted and implemented across multiple projects within the company, including War Commander, War Commander: Rogue Assault, and Vega Conflict.
